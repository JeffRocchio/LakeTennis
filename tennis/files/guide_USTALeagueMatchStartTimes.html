Captains-Please make sure that your matches start at the scheduled start time. Warm up and scorecard exchange should take place prior to match time. Read over the following procedures and let me know if you have questions. It is important that all matches start on time. Matches are staggered throughout the day on Saturdays and some weeknights...please be considerate of the teams that follow.

Thanks, Iris

----------------------
RULES:

  1. Captains should simultaneously exchange completed scorecards 15 minutes prior to the start time , even if weather and/or courts are questionable. A team match is started when the first ball, or any of the three or five positions (lines), is put into play. Prior to the start of a team match, if injury to, illness, or disqualification of a player occurs, the affected team may substitute a player who is immediately ready to play and may adjust their lineup at the affected position only. If no substitute is made within the 15-minute default period, the default will be awarded at that position. After the start of a team match, if injury to or illness to a player occurs, the opponents will be awarded default for that match only.
  2. Forfeits must be done by forfeiting the lowest positions first. A FORFEIT CAN ONLY BE GRANTED TO A PLAYER WHO IS PRESENT AND DRESSED TO PLAY.
  3. The 15 minute default rule is in effect. Example: Scheduled start time is 8:30 a.m. Teams should arrive for warm up by 8:15 a.m. Captains exchange scorecards and any players not present by scheduled start time are moved to the bottom positions, so that defaults will be from the bottom up. The #2 singles must be defaulted before #1 singles; #3 doubles must be defaulted before #2 or #1 doubles. If a team is missing two players, they may default #1 singles & #2 singles or #3 doubles. Missing players are moved to the bottom positions and will be defaulted at 8:45 a.m. if the players have not shown. All other positions should start on time and only the bottom position will be effected by the missing players.
