How Do I Enter Scores?

 

How do I enter a score once my team has completed a match?

   * Obtain the Match ID Number. You can get this from our roster grid. Click on the date of the match in the grid to see the match number.

   * Go to the USTA's TennisLink Web Site. (Tennis Link)

   * Click on the (tiny) Record a Score link on the right-hand side of the page. (Record a Score).

   * Enter your USTA number into box titled "USTA Number:"

   * Enter the match ID Number into box titled "Match Number:"

   * Click the 'NEXT' button and follow the directions from there.
