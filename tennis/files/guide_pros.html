   * Individual lesson, Full-Hr: $35.

   * Clinic, 5-8 people: $60 per hr.

For multi-person clinics the cost generally works out to be about $10 a person. Additionally, I like to cap the number of people at eight because after that it turns into too much standing around. i like to keep everyone involved and moving! Location wise, I'm open to anything, public courts or neighborhood courts, or wherever would work best for the group.

I've been teaching tennis on and off for the better part of ten years with interludes during college and while I was teaching high school history. I played tennis for UNCC from 1999-2002 and spent the following year working with the team as an assistant coach. I have also worked at various clubs around the Charlotte area, including North Charlotte Racquet Club and Skybrook.
